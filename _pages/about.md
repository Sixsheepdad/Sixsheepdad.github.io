Hi there! 👋

I'm **Liuzhiyang**, a master's student at the [School of Resource and Environment Engineering, Wuhan University](https://www.rs.whu.edu.cn/). My research interests lie in:

- 🛰️ **Multimodal Remote Sensing** — fusing optical, SAR, and other data modalities
- 👁️ **Computer Vision** — especially semantic segmentation and scene understanding
- 🗺️ **Geospatial AI** — bridging remote sensing with deep learning

I'm passionate about pushing the boundaries of how machines interpret Earth observation data. If you're working on similar problems, feel free to reach out!

## 🔬 Research Interests

- Semantic Segmentation of Remote Sensing Imagery
- Multimodal Data Fusion (Optical + SAR + Hyperspectral)
- Vision-Language Models for Remote Sensing
- Oil Spill Detection with Air-Space Remote Sensing
